\begin{frame}{Putting this all together:}
    \begin{itemize}
        \item Over the past decade, new wells have become increasingly deep.
        \item The current population of wells is aging. Over half of existing, unplugged wells are over 15 years old.
        \item This subpopulation of wells produce only about 12$\%$ of the state's oil production.
        \item Record production levels mean that \textbf{there may never be a better time to ensure that legacy wells are properly decommissioned.} 
    \end{itemize}
